Strawberry Infographic Poster

In this projects, I designed an Infographic Poster for Strawberry. The content included: English and Latin names for Headline; Fruit anatomy diagram with connectors; Benefits, Season, Taste, Calories; Digestion Elements circular bar chart; Nutrition Facts bar chart; Wellness / health human body diagram; selected data on World map market and growth; Recipe; Credits for text and images in the footer.
Target Population
People who needs information of Strawberry.
Skill
Graphic Design.
Tool
Adobe Illustrator.
Adobe Photoshop.

Concept

Sketch.


I sketched the overall layout for poster on two tabloids.


Famous Architects Alphabet Poster (Tabloid 11X17”).

Color Palette


I kept the color green and red of strawberry. However, because bright green and bright red are complementary harmony, I added black in them to reduce color contrast and picked another mild color- light brown as background color. To avoid dullness, I used texture everywhere: as backfround, wooden texture for headline, book texture for recipe, paper texture for map, coffee cup texture for circular chart, etc.






Typeface: Tahoma pairs with SansSerif.


When Tahoma pairs with SansSerif, the poster is readable and looks organised. To integrate as a whole, I kept consistent font sizes, leading, alignment and headlines hierarchy.
