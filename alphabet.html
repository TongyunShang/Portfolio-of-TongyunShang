Famous Architects Alphabet Poster

In this projects, I designed an English Alphabet Poster whose theme was Famouse Architects. I choosed the most widely-used names of architects, for example, Zaha- first name of Zaha Hadid and Khan- last name of Louis Khan. I picked black- the favorite color of architects as the basic color and script-look as the typeface. This poster is for everyone who is engaged or interested in Architecture including myself to know and remember those world famous architects.

Architects.
Designers.
Architecture buffs.
Skill

Concept

Color Palette


Architects like simple color and always dark color, such as black and gray. As a fan of Architecture, I wanted to keep this style of architects but added some lively bright colors to avoid dullness. I first looked through many color palettes on Adobe Color CC and choosed this classical one below as my basic color pallete. And generated more colors based on Monochromatic Harmony.


Typeface: Segoe Print.


Architects are doing sketches all the time, so I wanted the font to be like their handwriting letters. Font Segoe Print is styled and is based on handwriting letters, and at the same time it is clear to read.

Design Process

Sketches.


I sketched the overall layout and color arrangement of this poster. Beside, I also sketched out the designs for 4 models.


Alphabet Sketches of Layout and Models.


Color Palette.

Models.


I first created models for N, W, I, A, O, and modules that contain longest (V for Van de Rohe) and shortest words (R for Rem). The first step after I selected the images of those architecs was masking those pictures with color palette and thick letters in Photoshop. And then I placed them in Illustrator and kept consistent layout for each model, including the alignment of capital letters, words and masked images.
