MODERN TRENDS

I include big hero images on the top of the page, video content and relevant images for each section. By utilizing other trends, I use minimal text, sliders, organized but not stubborn arrangements for various content, and liberal free space. Additionally, I encompass social media icons in three different places, one is in the “share” drop down menu in navigation, another one is in the “Social Media Feeds”, and the last one is in footer. Furthermore, calling for “GIVE” is also very necessary, so I put the GIVE button on the very top.


TYPEFACE

Century Gothic and Myriad pro.
The typeface I choose are Century Gothic and Myriad pro, because the first looks neat and modern style as title while the second one is more readable for visitors.
