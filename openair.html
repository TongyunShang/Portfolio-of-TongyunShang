Summit IDG Web Redesign

In this team project, we redesigned the Summit IDG website using Wordpress template. My role was to build framework for each page, study how to use plug-ins and then build the homepage. During this process, I mainly focused on the Accessibility Web Design: aria tags, color contrast, alternative text, etc. Additionally, I followed the modern trend to include hero image, free space, minimal text, sliders. social media icons. etc.

Homepage

I added a hero image on the top with their logotype and messages on it, conveying clear identity emphasis. I moved the Upcoming Events from side bar to the center, reducing the maxima from 10 to 3, with a Google Map showing event location on the right. I removed the Recent Donation section from sidebar, Blog and Atheletes from Navigation, added Facebook Feed on the right side and Honor 22 as our client requested. I changed the wording from "Apply Now!" to "Get Involved", none "Donations" to verb. "Donate", calling for action. Besides, I added "War Affected Mind" and tagline "To Support, Aid, and Honor" under orgnisation name to emphasize their mission. To reduece the repeated information of social media icons, I only kept facebook and twitter icons and moved them to the navigation for emphasis. For the Athletes Introduction section, I reduced the overall numbers, enlarged the images, cut off text, making information clearer and more readable.
As for overall style, I made the homepage a shorter scrolling page with less text, more free space, clear color contrast and invisible accessible design.
