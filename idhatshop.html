OVERALL STYLE

Minimalism.
I wanted to design a logo which could be very styled and keep some of their original elements. I looked at their current business card, and thus I wanted to keep their dark blue, red colors and the woman on this card. As our client wants to have a logo of clean and modern style at the same time, I was trying to simplify the woman on their business card to be more like a logo, and added modern elements on it -- the Monotype Corsiva typeface.


TYPEFACE

Monotype Corsiva.
I choose Monotype Corsiva as the font on logo, which is more complex compared to the logo itself. Because I think simple logo pairs with a little bit complex typeface can maintain the balance. And this typeface is like hand-written but more readable, as well as looks modern. The font-size of this signature is 90pt, which is readable but not being emphasized too much.
