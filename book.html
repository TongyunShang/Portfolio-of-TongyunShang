PROBLEM STATEMENT

I created an application that enables students in UMich to find inexpensive used books, or sell idled books to others.



DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

User-Centered design.



OVERVIEW SOLUTION



KEY FREATURE 1:

Interests.


Users will be asked to selet their interest topics when registered. Then the homepage will be tailerd depending on their interests, on which useres can scroll down to see all interests categories or swipe to see all the books under this interest. Of course they can edit interests and check all the books under each later on.

KEY FREATURE 2:

Map.


When have chosen one book from the bookshelf, users will be led to a list of all the users who have this book. With a click on the map icon, the location of those users will be shown on a map. Then they can see details of each individual book.
