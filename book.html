BooksMap Mobile APP

In this project, I prototyped an application that enables students in UMich to find inexpensive used books, or sell idled books to others. There are three unique features of this APP: using map to locate nearby users and contact them; providing 2 options for users to post or search information via Have or Need; pricing/paying with a dinner/concert/movie, etc., which both buyers and sellers can accept or not.

Problem Statement

Students often find books through three methods: borrowing in libraries, buying in bookstores or purchasing online. However, books in libraries are often in shortage, those in bookstores might be expensive, and it takes time shipping if purchased online. What if students want to get books instantly and in proper cost? Meanwhile, there are some students who have trouble dealing with their idled books, which still look new but wouldnâ€™t be used anymore. So how could those students meet with each other?

Solution Overview

What I am trying to do is providing those students an access to inexpensive books as fast as possible while others dealing with their idled books. I will create an application, where both sides of students can share their information of books they have/want, prices they feel comfortable with and locations they would like to exchange the books, etc. By doing so, they can reach the nearest users who have or need a book and would like to meet with them. This application would only work when there are a lot of users, so I decide to firstly narrow down the community to just students on central campus, where larger amount of students stay. But my target community is not limited to students on central campus.
